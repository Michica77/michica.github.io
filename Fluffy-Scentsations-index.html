About Us

We are a family oriented company that came about when
two friends discovered their love for crafting handmade products.
We first created soaps and candles with amazing yummy fragrances, hence the name "Fluffy Scentsations".
So in 2019, Fluffy Scentsations was born.
We also create lotions/creams, glass engravings and other crafts and put a smile on your face.
